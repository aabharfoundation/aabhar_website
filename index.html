About Raju Aaba Harde

Raju Aaba Harde was a visionary leader, an extraordinary philanthropist, and a tireless advocate for the community's welfare. Born in Galleborgaon, he dedicated his life to empowering the rural populace through education, sustainable farming practices, and healthcare initiatives. His unwavering commitment to helping the underprivileged continues to inspire countless lives. His tragic passing left a void, but his legacy lives on through the work of the AabHar Foundation.

From setting up scholarships for students to organizing health camps and supporting local farmers, Raju Aaba Harde believed in the transformative power of collective action. His efforts laid the groundwork for sustainable growth in the community, and his story serves as a testament to the power of resilience, compassion, and determination.

Our Mission

AabHar Foundation is dedicated to empowering communities through education, health initiatives, and sustainable agriculture. Inspired by the visionary Raju Aaba Harde, we strive to create a brighter future by uplifting underprivileged groups and fostering self-reliance.

Our Projects

Educational Scholarships

Providing financial assistance to students from marginalized communities to help them achieve their academic dreams.

Healthcare Camps

Organizing free medical camps in rural areas to ensure access to basic healthcare for all.

Sustainable Farming

Supporting local farmers with innovative techniques and resources for improved yield and sustainability.

Our Journey

2020: Establishing the Foundation

The foundation was established to carry forward the vision of Raju Aaba Harde, focusing on community welfare and development.

2021: Launch of Educational Initiatives

Scholarships were introduced for deserving students in rural areas.

2022: Expanding Healthcare Programs

Health camps were conducted, benefitting thousands in need of basic medical facilities.

2023: Promoting Sustainable Agriculture

Workshops and resources for local farmers were provided, helping them adopt eco-friendly practices.
